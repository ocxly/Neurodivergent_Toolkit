🧘 Grounding Tool

The 5-4-3-2-1 grounding exercise can help bring attention back to the present moment during overwhelm or anxiety.

5 Things You Can See

Look around slowly and name five visible objects.

4 Things You Can Feel

Notice textures, temperature, or physical sensations.

3 Things You Can Hear

Focus on nearby and distant sounds.

2 Things You Can Smell

Notice any scents in your environment.

1 Thing You Can Taste

Focus on any taste currently present.
